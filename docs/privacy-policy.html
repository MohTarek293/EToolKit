Documentation Privacy Policy EULA

EToolKit (“the Add-in”) is a desktop add-in for Autodesk Revit developed by Mohamed Tarek (“Developer”, “we”, “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use EToolKit.

1. Key Points

  • No telemetry or analytics are collected by the Developer.
  • No Revit model content is transmitted to the Developer.
  • Optional features may open or interact with third-party services (Autodesk, Google, GitHub), which operate under their own policies.

2. Information the Developer Does Not Collect

The Developer does not collect, store, or process:

  • Revit model files or model content
  • Personal information (such as name, email, phone number)
  • Usage analytics/telemetry controlled by the Developer

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Autodesk Entitlement API (Licensing Validation)

If EToolKit is distributed through the Autodesk App Store with trial/subscription licensing, the Add-in may validate your entitlement by calling Autodesk’s entitlement endpoint:

https://apps.autodesk.com/webservices/checkentitlement

Data involved may include your Autodesk signed-in user identifier (e.g., LoginUserId) and the AppId used for validation. This processing is performed by Autodesk, not the Developer, and is governed by Autodesk’s privacy statement and terms.

3.2 Feedback / Feature Requests (Google Forms)

EToolKit may include optional buttons that open a feedback or feature request form in your browser (e.g., Google Forms). Submitting any form is voluntary. Any information you provide is processed by the third-party provider and/or form owner under their policies.

3.3 Documentation Hosting (GitHub Pages)

Documentation and policy pages may be hosted using GitHub Pages. When you visit these pages, GitHub may receive standard web request information (such as IP address, user agent, and request logs) as part of normal website operation.

4. Local Storage

EToolKit may store a local entitlement cache on your device to reduce repeated license checks and improve performance. Example location:

%LOCALAPPDATA%\EToolKit\entitlement_cache.json

This cache is stored locally on your machine and is not sent to the Developer.

5. Security

EToolKit does not include spyware, malware, or tracking code. Network requests (such as entitlement validation) are made over HTTPS.

6. Children’s Privacy

EToolKit is not intended for individuals under the age of 18.

7. Changes to This Policy

The Developer may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect the latest version.
